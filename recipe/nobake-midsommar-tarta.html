ALGORITHM No-Bake Midsommar tårta

INPUT
- 300 g cream cheese (Philadelphia)
- 300 g cold heavy cream
- 100 g powdered sugar
- 1½ tsp vanilla extract
- ±300 g cookies
- Whole milk (for dipping)
- Sprinkles (for decoration)

PROCESS

1. In large bowl:
      Combine cream cheese + powdered sugar + vanilla extract + heavy cream.
      Whip UNTIL stiff peaks form.
      Stop mixing when cream holds its shape.

2. Select serving dish or cake board.

3. FOR each cookie layer (total: 4 layers):
      Dip each cookie in milk (≈5 seconds).
      Arrange cookies to form base layer.
      Add even layer of cheesecake cream.
   END FOR

4. Frost cake:
      Cover top and sides completely with remaining cream.

5. Refrigerate:
      Chill ≥ 4 hours (preferably overnight).
      Do NOT add sprinkles yet.

6. Before serving:
      Remove from fridge 30 minutes in advance.
      Decorate with sprinkles.
      Slice and serve.

STORAGE
- Keep covered in refrigerator.
- Shelf life: 5–7 days.

OPTIONAL MODULE
- Add a thin layer of (strawberry) jam between cookie layers.
- Adjust cake size by changing number of cookies per layer.

OUTPUT
- Soft, layered no-bake cookie cake.
- Creamy texture with milk-softened cookie layers.
