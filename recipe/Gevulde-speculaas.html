ALGORITHM Gevulde Speculaas (Dutch Spiced Almond Pastry)

INPUT
250 g all-purpose flour
150 g light brown sugar
125 g unsalted butter
10 g speculaas spice mix
1 tsp baking powder
Pinch of salt
25 ml milk

125 g almonds
125 g granulated sugar
Zest of ¼ organic lemon
1 egg white

20–25 whole almonds
1 egg yolk
1 tbsp milk or heavy cream

PROCESS

Combine flour + sugar + butter + spice mix + baking powder + salt + milk
Mix UNTIL cohesive dough forms.
Refrigerate ≥ 30 minutes.

Pulse almonds UNTIL finely ground (do not over-process).
Add sugar + lemon zest + egg white.
Pulse briefly UNTIL almond paste forms.

Preheat oven to 180°C.
Prepare baking dish (grease or line with parchment).

Divide dough INTO 2 equal portions.
Roll out first portion to fit dish.
Place into dish as base layer.

Spread almond paste evenly over dough.

Roll out second portion.
Place over filling.
Seal edges gently.

Whisk egg yolk + milk.
Brush top layer.
Arrange whole almonds on top.
Brush again with egg mixture.

Bake 40 minutes at 180°C.
Cool completely before slicing.

OPTIONAL
Create homemade speculaas spice mix:
8 tbsp cinnamon
2 tbsp nutmeg
2 tbsp ground cloves
1 tbsp ground ginger
2 tsp mace
2 tsp cardamom

Mix thoroughly and store airtight.

OUTPUT
Golden-brown spiced pastry with soft almond center.
Best sliced when fully cooled.
