Algorithm: Gevulde Speculaas

(Dutch Spiced Almond Pastry)


--> INPUT Ingredients

Dough

  • 250 g all-purpose flour
  • 150 g light brown sugar
  • 125 g unsalted butter
  • 10 g speculaas spice mix*
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 25 ml milk

Almond Filling

  • 125 g almonds
  • 125 g granulated sugar
  • Zest of ¼ organic lemon
  • 1 egg white

Topping

  • 20–25 whole almonds
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp milk or heavy cream

--> PROCESS (Preparation)

  1. Combine all dough ingredients and mix until a cohesive dough forms.
  2. Wrap the dough and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Pulse almonds in a food processor until finely ground (do not over-process).
  4. Add sugar, lemon zest, and egg white. Pulse briefly until combined.
  5. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  6. Line a baking dish with parchment paper or grease lightly.
  7. Divide chilled dough into two equal portions.
  8. Roll out first portion to fit the baking dish.
  9. Place dough into dish.
  10. Spread almond paste evenly over base layer.
  11. Roll out second portion of dough.
  12. Place over filling and gently seal edges.
  13. Whisk egg yolk with milk or cream.
  14. Brush top layer with egg mixture.
  15. Arrange whole almonds on top.
  16. Brush again with egg mixture.
  17. Bake for 40 minutes at 180°C.
  18. Cool completely before slicing.

*Optional Module: Speculaas Spice Mix

    If you want to create your own spice mix:
  • 8 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp ground cloves
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 2 tsp mace
  • 2 tsp cardamom

    • Mix thoroughly and store in an airtight container.

--> OUTPUT

A golden-brown, fragrant spiced pastry with a soft almond center. Best served after fully cooled to allow clean slicing and optimal texture.
