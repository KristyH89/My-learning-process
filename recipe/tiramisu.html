Algorithm: Tiramisu

INPUT Ingredients

  • 1 container mascarpone
  • 1 container heavy cream
  • Approximately 120 g sugar
  • 1–2 tsp almond extract
  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • 1–2 cups coffee, brewed
  • Cocoa powder, for decoration

PROCESS (Preparation)

  1. Brew coffee.
  2. Transfer coffee to dipping bowl.
  3. Add almond extract to coffee.

  4. Whip mascarpone + heavy cream + sugar UNTIL stiff and creamy.

  5. Select deep serving dish.

  6. Layer mascarpone mixture (base layer).

  7. FOR each layer of ladyfingers:
    Dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture (2-3 seconds).
    Place over mascarpone layer.
    Add new layer of mascarpone mixture.
    END FOR

  8. Finish with mascarpone layer on top.

  9. Cover dish.
  10. Refrigerate ≥ 4 hours.

  11. Before serving:
    dust with cocoa powder.

OUTPUT

Chilled, layered tiramisu ready to serve.

Optional Modules

  • Replace almond extract with amaretto if desired.
  • Reduce sugar to taste.
  • Add a layer of strawberry jam between the ladyfinger layers.
  • Top with fresh strawberries for decoration.
